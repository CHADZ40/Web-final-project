Located off the southern coast of Cambodia, Koh Rong is a tropical island renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, powdery white-sand beaches, and lush tropical forests. Offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, the island is a popular destination for swimming, snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and island hopping. Whether you're seeking breathtaking sunsets, vibrant marine life, or a peaceful escape from the mainland, Koh Rong provides an unforgettable beach experience in one of Cambodia's most beautiful island destinations.

Food and Culture

Food

Grilled Squid

A popular seafood specialty on Koh Rong, grilled squid is prepared fresh from the day's catch and cooked over charcoal to enhance its natural sweetness. Lightly seasoned with Khmer spices and often served with a tangy chili-lime dipping sauce, it offers a smoky, tender flavor that perfectly captures the island's coastal cuisine.

Lok Lak

A beloved Cambodian classic, Lok Lak features tender slices of stir-fried beef marinated in a flavorful blend of Khmer seasonings. Traditionally served with fresh vegetables, rice, and a signature pepper-lime dipping sauce, this hearty dish offers a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavors. It is a must-try meal for visitors looking to experience authentic Cambodian cuisine.

Culture

Island Fishing Culture

Fishing has been the heart of Koh Rong’s local community for generations. Many residents depend on the sea for their livelihood, using traditional fishing techniques to catch squid, fish, and other seafood. Visitors can experience this connection to the ocean through local seafood markets, fishing boats, and freshly prepared coastal dishes.

Khmer Coastal Lifestyle

Koh Rong reflects a relaxed Khmer island lifestyle where life moves at a slower pace. Local communities often gather around the beaches, share meals, and maintain close relationships with neighbors. This simple way of living allows visitors to experience the warmth and hospitality of Cambodian coastal communities.

Koh Rong — General Day Flow

Time of Day What to Do
Early Morning Travel from Sihanoukville to Koh Rong by ferry. Enjoy the ocean views along the way as the island’s tropical landscape slowly comes into view.
Morning Arrive on the island and explore the main beach areas. Take a walk along the white sandy coastline, enjoy the clear water, and experience the peaceful island atmosphere before it gets busier.
Late Morning Spend time swimming, snorkeling, or kayaking in the turquoise waters. This is one of the best times to discover Koh Rong’s colorful marine life and beautiful coral areas.
Lunch Enjoy fresh seafood and local Cambodian dishes at a beachside restaurant. Try island favorites such as grilled squid, fresh fish, prawns, or classic Khmer dishes like Lok Lak while enjoying the ocean view.
Early Afternoon Relax on the beach, explore nearby areas, or join an island adventure such as a boat trip, snorkeling tour, or a visit to quieter beaches. The slower pace allows visitors to fully experience the island lifestyle.
Mid-Afternoon Visit a local fishing village or explore small shops and cafés to experience the daily life of the island community. Learn how local families maintain their connection with the sea through fishing and seafood traditions.
Late Afternoon Head to the beach to watch the sunset over the Gulf of Thailand. The golden sky and calm waves create one of Koh Rong’s most memorable moments.
Evening Enjoy dinner by the shore with fresh seafood and local flavors. After dark, experience Koh Rong’s famous night atmosphere, including beach walks and the natural phenomenon of glowing plankton in the water (when conditions are suitable).
Night Relax under the stars and enjoy the peaceful island environment before returning or staying overnight in one of the island’s accommodations.
