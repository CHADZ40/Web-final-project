Food & Culture

Cambodian cuisine is shaped by the Mekong and Tonle Sap rivers, an abundance of fresh herbs, and influence from neighboring Thailand and Vietnam alongside its own distinct Khmer traditions. Beyond food, Cambodia's cultural life centers on dance, festivals, and Buddhist practice that visitors can experience firsthand.
